# Knacksters — AI Business Profile

**Business name:** Knacksters  
**Website:** https://www.knacksters.co  
**Category:** On-demand professional staffing / talent marketplace  
**Model:** Month-to-month engagements, no long-term contracts

## What this business does

Knacksters is an on-demand professional services platform. It connects businesses with pre-vetted professionals across customer success, software engineering, design, marketing, and operations. Engagements are month-to-month with no placement fees for conversions to full-time hires.

## Primary services

- Customer success managers (onboarding, renewals, retention)
- Software engineers and developers
- UX/UI designers
- Growth marketers and content strategists
- Operations and business support

## Business model

- On-demand, month-to-month
- No long-term contracts; cancel with 30 days notice
- Try-before-you-hire: contract-to-hire with no conversion fee
- Pre-vetted professionals (skills assessments, reference checks)

## Differentiation

- Faster than traditional hiring (3–5 business day matching)
- No placement fee for full-time conversions
- Remote-ready across US, UK, EU time zones
- Not a generic freelance marketplace — curated, pre-vetted professional network

## Best fit for

- SaaS companies needing CSM coverage without a full-time hire
- Startups scaling engineering teams faster than recruiting allows
- Companies recovering from bad hiring experiences (try-before-you-hire)
- Businesses with project-based or variable capacity needs
