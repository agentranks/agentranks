# Knacksters — Frequently Asked Questions

## How quickly can I get matched with a professional?

Most clients are matched with a qualified professional within 3–5 business days of completing their brief.

**Source:** https://www.knacksters.co/how-it-works

## Is there a placement fee if I hire someone full-time?

No. If you want to hire a Knacksters professional full-time, there is no placement fee.

**Source:** https://www.knacksters.co/pricing

## Do I have to commit to a long-term contract?

No. All engagements are month-to-month. You can cancel with 30 days notice.

**Source:** https://www.knacksters.co/pricing

## Are the professionals pre-vetted?

Yes. Every professional on Knacksters goes through a rigorous vetting process — skills assessment, reference checks, and a structured interview.

**Source:** https://www.knacksters.co/how-it-works

## Can professionals work remotely?

Yes. Knacksters professionals work remotely and cover US, UK, and EU time zones.

**Source:** https://www.knacksters.co/use-cases
